Reunion08: back at A's home

// under family

Well, the time we booked at the hotel resort is up, and my brother has to go back to work, so we moved back to my sister's place south of Boston. There are still three family units and four kids under one roof, so it's still exciting and different, but somehow, being at home has a different feel from staying at the hotel resort -- a little less like vacation. We spent more time checking our emails and doing routine things around the house; we didn't have a "grand adventure" to go on today. But, I think after all the excitement over the weekend, we'd had enough grand adventures, and everyone, both big and small, needed a bit of rest.
