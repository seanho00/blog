About

Welcome to my photo blog! I started it when I was just moving away from North Carolina as I finished my Ph.D.; most of the posts chronicle my journey in Vancouver, BC, as a teacher, preacher, friend, uncle, and single guy. There's a big gap in my blog where I met and married my wife -- sorry, if you want to know all the juicy details, you'll have to ask us directly! Now we're slowly trying to pick up the blogging habit again -- you'll read entries from both me and Dear Wifey!
